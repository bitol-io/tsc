# RFC-0041: Synonyms

RFC-0041 is a shared RFC: the same `synonyms` field is added to both ODCS (v3.2.0) and ODPS (v1.1.0). It is maintained as a single document under ODCS v3.2.0.

See [`../odcs-v3.2.0/0041-synonyms.md`](../odcs-v3.2.0/0041-synonyms.md) for the canonical specification, including the ODPS-specific applicable levels (the Data Product itself and Output ports).

Approved by the TSC on 2026-06-01 for inclusion in ODPS v1.1.0.
